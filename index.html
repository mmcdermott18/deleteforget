Delete/Forget is an exhibition of leftovers, of what remains on a public computer after a user leaves. On any given day a computer can have several gigabits of external files on it or nothing on it. Factors such as whether the computer was restarted recently or if the last person to use it took the time to delete files and empty the trash come into play. This exhibition is a curated look at those files that remain. It is a look at appropriation and fair use of files on public computers. The ambiguous territory of leftovers is explored.
