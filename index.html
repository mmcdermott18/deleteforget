Delete/Forget

1 October 2014 — 28 February 2014

Everyday people sit down and use public computers. Generally there is no consideration for the previous user or the next user. At the time of use, the computer is mine and what I do on it is mine. But consistently we leave traces of what we used the computer for behind. Scans are left on the desktop. Downloads are left in the trash. Internet histories remain with the browser. This exhibition is a curated look at those files that remain. It is a look at appropriation and fair use of files on public computers and explores ambiguous territory of leftovers.
